Assessment

BEA's objective during the Assessment stage is to provide our clients with the information they need to evaluate the economic viability and sustainable goals of their project. The services provided in this stage include an understanding of our clients' objectives, energy usage analysis of utility bills, site and asset assessment analysis, project finance options including rebates and incentives, technology alternatives and potential savings.

Procurement

BEA can manage the procurement process for you. We assist our clients with the formation of project specifications and evaluation criteria for the RFP. We develop a procurement plan and methodology to achieve optimal results. We are vendor and technology neutral, which means we have no exclusive partnerships with manufacturers that would limit our recommendations.

Implementation

BEA will act as the owners representative and primary point of contact for our clients from concept through completion of construction and commissioning.
