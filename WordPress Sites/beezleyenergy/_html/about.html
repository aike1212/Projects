Beezley Energy Advisors combine economic, environmental and societal/human perspectives and considerations to arrive at the most comprehensive and long-lasting results.

Beezley Energy Advisors was established as a California Limited Liability Corporation in 2010 to address the need for Owners to get the best and brightest talent, experienced with renewable energy projects and sustainable initiatives, at affordable prices. Our focus is on transforming facilities into more sustainable operations that are more cost effective, operationally efficient and lower maintenance through focusing on energy, water and waste - to create more healthy environments for people, and to protect natural resources for present and future generations.

Our services include:

  • Owners Representation
  • Feasibility Assessments
  • Project Financing/ PPA
  • Procurement Administration
  • Rebate and Financial Incentives
  • Construction Project Management
  • Project Commissioning
