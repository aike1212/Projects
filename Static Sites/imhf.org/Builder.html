Welcome to IMHF

The Indiana Masonic Home Foundation, located at, 525 N. Illinois St., Indianapolis, IN 46204, was incorporated in 1936 to provide long-term financial assistance to The Indiana Masonic Home. The Foundation continues to be the largest non-resident source of funds for The Home. 1-800-277-4643

What We Are

The Indiana Masonic Home Foundation is a not-for-profit (501) © (3) corporation whose objective is to provide financial support for the continuum of quality care to eligible members of The Indiana Masonic Home family.
